# Ending Guide — Midnight Sakura Neko

Spoiler-light route guidance for current build.

## Core stats
- `affinity` — emotional closeness with Yuna
- `honesty` — directness and emotional truth
- `courage` — willingness to choose hard things
- `myth_clue` — progress on Neko Hour mystery

## Festival trajectory (Chapter 4)
Your Chapter 4 choice sets final direction:
- `heart` → romance-forward finale
- `mind` → mystery/truth-forward finale
- `drift` → distance-forward finale

## Endings

### 1) Soft Dawn End — *Chosen On Purpose*
High-level path:
- Build closeness with Yuna (`affinity`)
- Choose emotionally direct responses (`honesty`)
- In Chapter 4, choose **Heart path**

### 2) Clocktower Truth End — *Dawn Contract / Unfinished Line*
High-level path:
- Investigate myths (`myth_clue`)
- Ask direct questions and follow clues
- In Chapter 4, choose **Mind path**
- Sub-outcome depends on final writing choice on the blank line

### 3) Moonlit Distance End — *Last Train Missed*
High-level path:
- Repeated postponement/avoidance
- Lower honesty and lower commitment choices
- In Chapter 4, choose **Hesitate and postpone**

## Fast route tips
- **Romance run:** prioritize Sakura Alley choices + direct apologies + intentional commitment.
- **Mystery run:** prioritize Lantern District investigations + clue-building + truth-first choices.
- **Distance run:** dodge, delay, rationalize.

## Replay recommendation
Do three runs in this order:
1. Heart
2. Mind
3. Drift

This reveals emotional arc, lore arc, then contrast arc.
