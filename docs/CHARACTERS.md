# Character Bible

## Yuna (Heroine)
- Archetype: playful anime catgirl with hidden melancholy
- Public vibe: teasing, agile, chaotic-cute
- Private truth: afraid of being needed only when convenient
- Core need: to be chosen intentionally, not accidentally
- Signature line style: witty one-liners that hide direct emotion

## Protagonist (Player POV)
- Archetype: newcomer with unresolved past
- Strengths: observant, kind when present
- Flaws: emotionally avoidant, overthinks timing
- Core arc: from passive drifting to deliberate commitment

## Supporting Cast (Planned)
- **Akari** (station attendant): knows city stories, speaks in riddles
- **Jun** (childhood friend): practical realist, challenges idealism
- **Reina** (festival organizer): social anchor, pushes action over rumination

## Relationship Variables (Planned)
- `affinity` — closeness with Yuna
- `honesty` — willingness to be direct
- `courage` — choosing difficult truth over comfort
- `myth_clue` — progress in unraveling Neko Hour legend
